Welcome to bashoneliners.com, a collection of shell script tips and tricks on a single line that can be very useful in GNU/Linux, UNIX, BSD, and similar systems. Post your cool bash one-liners to share with the world, or search our database of existing one-liners contributed by others.

Shell scripting can be an ugly business. It is easy to write quick and dirty hacks that only a few hardcore hackers understand. Our goal is to gather nicely written, clean, well-explained one-liners that conform to today's best practices, and do something useful in real life. Or just pure awesome and wicked cool, that's ok too.

To get the latest bash one-liners, follow our rss feed or @bashoneliners on Twitter. If you discover bugs, you can either report it on GitHub, or send an email to our public mailing list, or send us a private email.

The site is still pretty far from what we want it to be. Mainly we need more Django coders. At the minimum it would be great help if you could spread the word and talk about this site on Twitter, Google+, Facebook, all your social outlets. If you want to help with coding in Django, please take a look at our Source code page.
